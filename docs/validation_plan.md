# Validation Plan

## Objective

Determine whether the selected clamp force reliably produces:

- Die GEL 8010 bondline: 0.005 in
- Copper-spreader GEL 75 bondline: 0.025 in

## Suggested test matrix

Test several clamp forces around the nominal 4 lbf value, for example:

- 2 lbf
- 3 lbf
- 4 lbf
- 5 lbf
- 6 lbf

Repeat each force at least three times.

## Record

- Applied force
- Initial dispense mass or volume
- Initial bondline thickness
- Final bondline thickness
- Assembly temperature
- Dwell time
- Surface finish
- Alignment
- Observed edge squeeze-out
- Rebound after unloading
- Photos before and after compression

## Measurement methods

Possible methods include:

- Mechanical thickness stops
- Feeler gauges
- Micrometer measurements of the full stack
- Cross-sectioning sacrificial samples
- Mass-based dispense verification
- Calibrated compression fixture with a load cell

## Model update

Once enough measurements exist:

1. Plot final bondline thickness versus average pressure.
2. Plot final bondline thickness versus force.
3. Separate loading and unloading data if rebound occurs.
4. Fit an empirical curve only if the measured relationship is repeatable.
5. Report confidence intervals and measurement uncertainty.
